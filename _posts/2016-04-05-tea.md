hot tea

flies falls in

i stare, drink it slowly

when a fly falls in our tea, why do we recoil

this and not that, what falsehood

flies, tea, sand, man, all parts, one system

the design of the cosmos, is infinitely more complex

than our own designs, which of course are but a subset

of the designs of the cosmos, which are naught
